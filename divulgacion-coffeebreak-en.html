Coffee Break: Señal y Ruido

The weekly discussion panel reviewing the latest scientific news. The leading science podcast in Spanish covering astrophysics, cosmology, and space sciences.

The Concept: Signal and Noise

The title of this podcast perfectly represents the technical and philosophical core of space science and, in a very special way, of our practical passion for astrophotography.

In Astrophotography

Processing in PixInsight is a constant battle for Signal-to-Noise Ratio (SNR). We calibrate and stack frames to mitigate thermal noise and CMOS sensor read noise, with the sole purpose of revealing the faint electromagnetic signal of ancient photons coming from emission nebulae or distant spiral galaxies.

In Astrophysics

Cutting-edge science operates under the same principle. Scientists and astrophysicists analyze terabytes of raw data from ground-based observatories and space telescopes like the James Webb. Their task is to program algorithms capable of discriminating true cosmological signals from instrumental disturbances and background noise.

The podcast Coffee Break: Señal y Ruido represents the perfect bridge between the technical tools we use at CabraSpace (such as the PixelMath-teca or PixInsight) and the pure physical-scientific understanding of the processes occurring in the celestial objects we capture in our deep-sky photos.

Note: This podcast is broadcast in Spanish, but serves as an outstanding conceptual and research resource for scientific papers and discoveries worldwide.
